## ARTICLE DEVELOPMENT INSIGHTS

**Queries that worked well:**
- "What's the specific decision being made and when?"
- "Who's making this decision and what do they need to know?"
- "What can field teams actually influence here?"
- "What's the competitive advantage if they act first?"
- "What data validates this isn't hype?"

**Revisions that strengthened articles:**
- Removing instructional "how-to" sections in favor of tactical implementation examples
- Adding specific product examples tied to actual client portfolios
- Clarifying decision timelines and phases vs. vague "soon" language
- Replacing "train your team" with specific scripts/tactics
- Acknowledging what customers already researched before arriving
- Connecting consumer trends to specific field team actions within scope of influence

**Content that got rejected/refocused:**
- Generic demographic observations without field marketing angle
- "Everyone should do this" vs. "This is your competitive advantage window"
- Standard industry trends vs. novel angles with 30-60 day action horizons
- Unsourced urgency claims (verified all tariff dates, tax credit deadlines, etc.)
- Articles suggesting field teams could change pricing, create bundles, or make strategy decisions
